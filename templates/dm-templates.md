# DM Templates - Twitter Outreach

## DM 1: Initial Outreach (Short)

**Template:**

Hey {{name}}! Love your content on {{topic}}. Quick question - have you thought about launching a digital product for your {{follower_count}} followers? I help creators do exactly that (I handle everything, we split profits). Interested?

---

## DM 2: Initial Outreach (With Compliment)

**Template:**

Hey {{name}}! Your recent post about {{specific_post}} was spot on. I noticed you have {{follower_count}} followers but no product yet. I help creators launch ebooks/courses - I create everything, you approve. 50/50 split. Want to hear more?

---

## DM 3: Follow-up (After no response)

**Template:**

Hey {{name}}, just bumping this. I'm helping a few creators launch products this month. Your audience would love a {{niche}} guide. Zero work on your end - I handle everything. Interested?

---

## DM 4: Value First

**Template:**

Hey {{name}}! I made a sample {{product_type}} in your niche to show you what's possible. Want me to send it? No strings attached - just want to show you what your audience is missing.

---

## DM 5: Social Proof

**Template:**

Hey {{name}}! Just helped a creator in your niche make $3K in a week with a digital product. Your audience is bigger - you could do even better. I handle all the work. 50/50 split. Want in?

---

## DM 6: Quick Question

**Template:**

Hey {{name}} - quick question. If I created a digital product for your audience and handled the entire launch, would you be interested in splitting profits? Zero work for you.

---

## DM 7: Last Chance

**Template:**

Hey {{name}}, final chance - I'm only taking 2 more creators this month. Your {{follower_count}} followers are waiting for a product. I'll create it, launch it, split profits. Yes or no?

---

## Response Templates

**When they say YES:**

> Awesome! Let's do this.
>
> Quick questions:
> 1. What's your audience's biggest pain point?
> 2. Email or DM for updates?
> 3. When are you free for a quick chat?
>
> I'll start on your product today!

**When they ask "what's the catch?":**

> No catch! Here's the deal:
>
> - I create the product (ebook/course)
> - You approve it
> - You share with your audience
> - We split profits 50/50
>
> I make money when you make money. Zero risk for you.

**When they say NOT NOW:**

> Totally get it. Timing matters.
>
> Offer stands whenever you're ready. Just reply to this DM.
>
> Keep creating great content!

---

## Best Practices

1. **Always personalize** - Reference something specific they posted
2. **Keep it short** - Under 200 characters
3. **No links first DM** - Builds trust
4. **Follow up once** - Don't spam
5. **Be genuine** - They can smell fake instantly
